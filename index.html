What I enjoy about it

  • I enjoy the ability to make something from nothing
  • Most of the people you work with love what they do
  • It's pretty well paid

  • I get to work with my friends

What I don't enjoy about it

  • There can be some pressure around deadlines

  • Not good for fitness
  • eyesight/posture problems

What working from home is like

  • Plan my own time
  • Free to take breaks whenever
  • Work longer days
  • Harder to switch off
  • I get to enjoy the sunshine!

What it is to be a freelancer

  • You work for yourself
  • You go to find work from other people
  • Finding and managing work is now your responsibility
  • You calculate your own tax
  • All your time is money
  • You can make time for things like this!
